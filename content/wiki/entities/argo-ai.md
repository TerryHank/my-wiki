# Argo AI

**Argo AI** was an autonomous vehicle technology company that developed the [[argoverse-dataset]], a critical resource for research in self-driving cars. The company was a joint venture between Ford and Volkswagen before being shut down by Ford in late 2022.

## Legacy

Despite its closure, Argo AI's contribution to the research community remains significant through the Argoverse dataset. The dataset continues to be a benchmark for:
- [[autonomous-driving-tasks|3D Object Tracking]]
- Motion Forecasting
- Stereo Depth Estimation

## Data Availability Impact

The shutdown of Argo AI had a direct impact on the accessibility of its research assets. The original hosting infrastructure for the Argoverse dataset on **Amazon S3** was decommissioned. Consequently, the dataset is no longer available for automated script-based downloads and must be manually retrieved from a **Google Drive** link. This transition highlights the risks of relying on corporate-hosted datasets for long-term scientific reproducibility.
