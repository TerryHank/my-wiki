# Support Vector Machines (SVMs)

**Support Vector Machines (SVMs)** are supervised machine learning algorithms used for classification and regression tasks. In computer vision, they were historically the state-of-the-art method before the rise of deep learning.

## Role in CIFAR-10 Benchmarking
SVMs are frequently cited in the context of the [[cifar-10-dataset]] as a baseline traditional machine learning algorithm. Comparing SVM performance against [[convolutional-neural-networks]] on CIFAR-10 helps illustrate the advantages of deep feature learning over hand-crafted features in image classification tasks.
